Hello there!

My name is Lisa Brown, and I'm on an exciting journey of reinvention. Having spent several years as a dedicated Registered Veterinary Technician, caring for our furry friends, I decided it was time for a change. With a passion for problem-solving and a love for learning, I embarked on a new adventure: coding.

Balancing the responsibilities of homemaking with my pursuit of coding proficiency, I've immersed myself in the world of programming languages and technologies. It's been an exhilarating challenge, one that has ignited my creativity and pushed me to explore new horizons.

While my background in veterinary medicine provided a solid foundation in precision and attention to detail, my foray into coding has allowed me to tap into my analytical side and embrace the ever-evolving landscape of technology.

As I prepare to reenter the workforce, armed with newfound skills and a fresh perspective, I'm eager to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with like-minded professionals. With each line of code I write, I'm one step closer to realizing my dreams and making a meaningful impact in the world of technology.
